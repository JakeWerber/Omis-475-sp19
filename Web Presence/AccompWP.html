My Other Accomplishments

Sigma Nu

When I first came to NIU I had a friend in a fraternity called Sigma Nu. They convinced me to join which know is one of the best decisions I have ever made. I have made a lot of connections throughout my time at Sigma Nu. My greatest accomplishment at Sigma Nu was that I became what is now called "Head of General Membership". In this position if any non-executive members had a problem or wanted to talk they came to me so that I could go to the executive board and voice the complaints of any member. This was a huge step in my life, and I am very glad I was able to this.

Community Service

During my time at NIU I have been able to help my community through community service. I have had about 80 hours of community service while here at NIU. One of the community service projects that I am most proud of is called NIU cares day. At NIU cares day you are split into teams and then are assigned a place to go. I was assigned to go to a Dekalb museum where we spent the day pulling weeds, laying wood chips, and doing other chores around the property. I loved this one the most because you got to see the before and after and when you are done you are proud that you helped.

Other Accomplishments

The last accomplishment that I will talk about is my time in other organizations, I was in APICS and CAB. APICS is a Operational Management club here on campus where you tour warehouses and other production facilities. CAB stands for campus activity board which is a school organization that holds events for the students here at NIU. I am happy with my decision to joining the organization and help educate myself and help the students here at NIU have more fun while at school.
